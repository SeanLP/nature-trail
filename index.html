Why Join?

Physical and Mental Benefits

Hiking improves fitness, builds muscle and strengthens bones. It has been shown that walking on uneven terrain can increase the amount of energy used by up to 28% compared to walking on flat terrain. Hiking improves blood flow and oxygen to the brain which enhances several areas of the brain. These include the areas responsible for concentration, focus, memory and happiness. Some research has shown that spending time outdoors increases attention spans and creative problem-solving skills by as much as 50%.
